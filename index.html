Value Engineering Suggestions

Alternative Materials

Consider replacing standard Aluminum with a high-strength Polycarbonate blend for the main housing. This reduces weight and trims material costs by 18% without affecting durability.

Logistics Optimization

Bulk shipping components locally instead of relying on decentralized overseas shipments can save approximately $3,000 per production cycle.

Component Consolidation

Incorporate the separate cooling fan controller directly into the main printed circuit board. This eliminates supplementary wiring and reduces labor assembly time drastically.
